Tax Rebate Information

Tax rebates are typically incentives or relief measures provided by the government. The Inflation Reduction Act was passed to attempt to encourage US-based manufacturing of green energy related infrastructure. The bill will provide at least $15 billion in tax credits to companies that invest in renewable energy-related properties such as manufacturing fuel cells, microturbines, or electric vehicle batteries. The tax credit rate ranges from 10-30% depending on the energy source type.
